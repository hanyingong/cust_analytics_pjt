2016-17 Term 3

HE Ptd Ltd: Converting Customer Data into Actionable Insights Through Customer Analytics Techniques


Project Team 5: The A Consulting Team
For Instructor: Seema Choksi
Description:
This is a web portal to document and showcase the analytical works for the group project, which aim to apply customer analytics techniques from the course on a bigger problem and dataset.

Version 1.0 : 29 May 2017 [draft01]

Objective of Project Work:

HE Ptd. Ltd. is a retail chain specialized in electronics. The company has implemented a CRM system to capture customer’s information and transactions.

With the main objective of maximizing profit, data in the CRM should be put into better use to provide actionable insights by focusing on customers (segments) with higher CLV.

Our team, “The A Consulting”, will assist HE Ptd. Ltd to conduct a POC by applying customer analytics techniques to discover insightful trends and patterns in the customer’s data and formulate actionable insights / strategies for customer acquisition and development – to meet the objective.
